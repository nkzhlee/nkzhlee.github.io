Bio

I am a research fellow assistant with Prof. JunXu since 2016. Before that, I obtained my bachelor degree in Nankai University (Tianjin, China), advised by Prof. Jie liu and Dr. Yuan Wang. Here is my CV.

My research interests Natural Language Processing, Information Retrieval and Machine Learning System. My work on Machine Reading Comprehension has been received at CCIR 2018. Moreover, I am the founder of a ML system call Easy Machine Learning and have served as core member for 3 years, and it has been stared 1681 until now. In addition, I focus on a document understanding program for Machine Reading Comprehension, the result will be submitted to AAAI 2019.
