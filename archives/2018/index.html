Bio

I am a research fellow assistant with Prof. JunXu since 2016. Before that, I obtained my bachelor degree in Nankai University (Tianjin, China), advised by Prof. Jie liu and Dr. Yuan Wang. Here is my CV.

My research interest resides at Reinforcement Learning and Nature Language Processing. And I am now focusing on Machine Reading Comprehension task, which based on Knowledge-based Deep Reinforcement Learning models. Moreover, I hope my research results can be applied to Machine Learning Applications in the Health Care and Sport realms and can benefit more and more people all over the world.

In addition, I am the founder of a open-source ML system called Easy Machine Learning and have served as core member for 3 years, and it has been stared 1700 on Github until now.
